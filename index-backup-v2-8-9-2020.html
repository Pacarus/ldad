Welcome to Linda D'Amico's Academy of Dance© and School of The Arts

Our mission

Linda D’Amico’s Academy of Dance is a family-friendly, local dance studio located in Pompton Plains, NJ. We are thrilled and humbled to open our doors for the local dancers of our community and share our love of dance! From our adorable Pre-K and Kinder ballerinas, to our beautiful recreational terpsichoreans, to our fierce national award winning competitors, we pride ourselves in making the love and passion of dance our number one priority!

"A river cuts through rock, not because of its power, but because of its persistence." —James N. Watkins.

Why choose LDAD© ?

LDAD© is more than just a place to come and learn new dance steps. Lifelong friendships are made here! Through confidence building classes, our students acquire advanced skills including; coordination, strength, grace and agility. Our dancers, through hard work and persistance are nurtured to create disciplined, charismatic young adults equipped with essential real-world skillsets needed to take on life’s journey no matter where it takes them!
